Abgedreht

The client project Abgedreht at the NEULAND. agency was about creating a digital image via an one-pager portfolio. The client said that it was important to him to make a good first impression and that all the information should be very compact, similar to a business card. I took care of the UI conception and the construction of a ready-designed prototype in Adobe XD. I wanted to create a more unusual look by incorporating a horizontal scroll. Another reason was to convey the feeling of a camera movement while looking through the page.
