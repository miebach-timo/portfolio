Storytelling & UX

This project was an important midterm exam at KISD. Our task was to theoretically work on a topic of our own choosing and to present it in a "practical" way. I decided on the hypothesis of how far storytelling can be combined with user experience design techniques. The result of my theoretical work was 5 criteria that can be used to evaluate digital products. I also used these criteria to build a fictional website as a prototype in Adobe XD. The whole theoretical work can be read here (German).
