Misery Of Lux

This was my very first project at KISD. Our task was to conceptualise, design and programme our own video game in Unity. In our group I worked on the level design and the overall look and feel. My group partner Aaron Pfaffenholz created various character designs as well as spritesheets. Misery of Lux is planned as a mystery jump and rum game, very much inspired by Little Nightmares and Limbo. The game tells the story of Lux, a princess and almost the last of her kind - because her country is at war with the shadow empire, who are sworn enemies of the light tribe and want to eradicate them. On the run, Lux tries to find and free her last tribemates, but encounters many obstacles on her way.
