The 3 Emperor

This project is purely personal. For a long time I had a story of my own that I always wanted to visualise. The compositions and character-designs are attempts to capture my ideas visually and tell small parts of the events happening in the plot. In short: The story is about three emperor who challenge the sovereignty of the gods over the mortal continent and plunge into a bitter war.
