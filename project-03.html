Magazine Culture

Magazine Culture was an academic seminar in KISD where we learned different editorial design theories and practices. In this seminar, our task was to design and print an issue of a fictional magazine. I decided to create a "designer magazine" that focuses on new design practices. I based my layout on a grid with a lot of negative space and large headlines to give a simple aesthetic impression.
