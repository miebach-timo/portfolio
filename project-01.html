Rad im Veedel

The client project Rad Im Veedel at the NEULAND. agency dealt with the development of an broader internet presence. It was particularly important to the client to increase their reach and improve their image on social media. My task was to analyse the existing site structure and to develop a new concept and implement it in an Adobe XD prototype. It was also significant for the client to convey a more modern and classy image in their branding. For this reason, I decided on a very reduced design and a dark colour scheme.
